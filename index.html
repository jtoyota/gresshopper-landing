We are building a platform where immigrant women can connect with mentors and peers who will support them achieve their personal and professional goals.

Visibility of each others challenges and achievements is a powerful tool to inspire us to and our dreams, educate ourselves, and pursue amazing careers. We believe that a place to ask questions, exchange experiences, and discover opportunities can help us be better equipped to fulfill our potential.

How it Works

Be a mentor

Maybe you don’t see yourself as a mentor right now, but your experience can hep others. When you signup as a mentor you will tell us about your background in five different areas that go beyond technical knowledge or a particular professional role. We will match you with someone who is looking for mentorship from someone like you. You can look at our suggestions and pick who you would like to advise.

Mentoring doesn't need to be a huge time commitment. The first step is to send your mentors an encouraging message. We will help you on every step of the way!

Be a mentee

Having a career in a new country is hard, but you don’t have to do it by yourself. Whether you need to get recertified to work in your field or looking for a career shift, you can connect with other professionals who transitioned to the US job market and you can learn from their experience.

We want to connect immigrant women with professional who can help them advance their careers. After you create your profile, we will match you with one of the mentors using our custom algorithm and provide you with tips on how to make the most of this relationship.
